Carrier Screening Decision Calculator

This program is designed to be used in situations when a child is found to be a carrier of a single pathogenic or likely pathogenic variant in an autosomal recessive gene. It will calculate whether it is more cost-effective to do single-gene analysis for the parents or to reflex to expanded carrier testing.
OR
Please enter either population carrier frequency or disease prevalence, not both. If both are entered, carrier frequency will be calculated from disease prevalence.
